We are a full-service commercial and residential renovation company based in Mississauga, ON. At First Option, we build structures and craft experiences and turn dreams into reality. With a legacy of excellence in the construction industry, we are dedicated to delivering top-notch construction solutions that meet and exceed your expectations.
